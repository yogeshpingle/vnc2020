6th International Conference on Information Engineering and Management (ICIEM 2020)

To be held at Bharti Vidyapeeth, New Delhi from 12th-14th March, 2020

Theme: Business Management & Health Informatics

Paper submission Link: http://bvicam.ac.in/inddiacom/submitPaper.asp

This Conference is a part of INDIACom-2020 (http://bvicam.ac.in/indiacom/). The Conference aims to grasp innovative ideas from researchers who focus mainly on using IT as a tool for adding value to organisations. The Conference welcomes papers in the fields of information systems and more precisely in the areas of information management, big data analytics, business processes modelling / engineering, project management, healthcare engineering and enterprise computing. Moreover, papers which address: business solutions, development methods (based on the use of models), design, analysis, synthesis, deployment, testing and maintenance of complex project environments with a view to produce quality solutions, that can contribute to corporate decision making at a strategic level are also welcome.

Every paper will be reviewed by at least two reviewers. All accepted papers of the Conference, after presentation, will be published in the main conference proceedings by IEEE Xplore, which is indexed with world’s leading Abstracting & Indexing (A&I) databases, including ISI, SCOPUS, DBLP, EI-Compendex, etc. All Conference papers will also be published by Bharti Vidyapeeth in the pre-conference proceedings to be made available at the time of registration.

In addition, selected papers presented to ICIEM2020 will be recommended, with substantial extension, for publication in the following journals:

  • Business and Information Systems Engineering (Thomson Reuters, 2016 Impact Factor 3.39)
  • International Journal of Information Technology & Decision Making (SCI Expanded/ISI 2016 Impact Factor 1.664), Scopus and many other databases
  • International Journal of Ambient Computing and Intelligence (IJACI), ISSN - 1941-6237. Indexed byThomson Reuters, SCOPUS, EI Compendex, etc
  • Australian Journal of Intelligent Information Processing Systems (Indexed by INSPEC, Ranked B by Australian Government)
